Our Story

From street culture to global phenomenon

UrbanKicks was born on the streets

Founded in 2018 by sneaker enthusiasts, UrbanKicks started as a small online store in New York City. What began with just 12 pairs of sneakers has grown into a global brand with over 500 unique styles.

Our mission is simple: bring premium, authentic sneakers to everyone who appreciates street culture and urban fashion. Every pair we sell comes with our authenticity guarantee.
